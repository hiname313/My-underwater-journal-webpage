My Underwater Journal

This is a coral reef, coral reefs are shallow coastal areas full of colourful rock called coral. In a coral reef you can find everything, from small fish to big reef sharks.




The biggest reef in the world is the great barrier reef located in Australia. It starts at north Austrailia and reaches till east Australia. But due to global warming and climate change all of these reefs have started to become dead and most coral in all of the reefs had lost their colour.

This is the Whale Shark, the biggest fish in the world. It is as long as a single decker bus. These fish are often found in the Indian ocean near Sri Lanka and Maldives. They are also found in some pacific locations such as Indonesia
